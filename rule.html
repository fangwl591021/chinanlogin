🧾 預約規則 Reservation Rules

  • 每班次限額 7 名乘客，額滿即無法再預約。Each trip is limited to 7 passengers; once full, no more reservations are accepted.
  • 每位學生每日限預約 1 班次，不得重複。Each student may book only one trip per day.
  • 請於乘車前至少 30 分鐘完成預約。Please complete your reservation at least 30 minutes before departure.
  • 若臨時無法搭乘，請於出發前取消，以釋出名額。If you cannot take the bus, please cancel before departure to release the seat.
  • 預約完成後將顯示於「我的預約」清單中。After successful booking, the trip will appear in your “My Reservations” list.
⚠️ 名額有限，請珍惜預約機會，勿惡意佔位。
⚠️ Limited seats — please reserve responsibly.

📱 注意事項 Important Notes

  • 請攜帶學生證以利身份確認。Please bring your student ID for verification.
  • 上車前請提前 5 分鐘到達集合地點。Arrive at the stop at least 5 minutes before departure.
  • 系統將於當日 23:50 關閉預約功能。Reservation system closes at 23:50 each day.
  • 乘車當日未出席者，系統將紀錄一次未搭乘紀錄。No-shows will be recorded in the system.
